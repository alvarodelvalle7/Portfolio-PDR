MatchWornShirt - Partnership & Kitman Event

Description

In this project, I documented an exclusive event organized by Matchwornshirt, featuring partnerships with various teams and presented by the legendary Ruud Gullit. The event showcased the collaborative efforts between Matchwornshirt and the teams, highlighting the contributions of Kitmen and other key figures. Through compelling visuals and engaging storytelling, we brought to life the dedication and passion behind these partnerships, emphasizing their importance in the world of football.

Roles

Edition – Color Grading – Filming – Production
