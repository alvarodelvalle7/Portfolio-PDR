Description

I’m an audiovisual storyteller with a deep passion for capturing emotions and telling visual stories that move people. Whether it’s through video, photography, or music, I love creating content that feels authentic and engaging.

My background spans sports, music, and short-form content, but at the core, I’m driven by creativity, culture, and connection. I’m always looking for new ways to grow, collaborate, and take my craft to the next level — approaching every project with energy, purpose, and a commitment to making something truly memorable.
