FIFPRO Event - Working session

Description

In a recent project, I worked on a recap video for a major FIFPRO event, showcasing the organization's global efforts in advancing players' rights and well-being. The event gathered football professionals, union representatives, and key stakeholders to discuss the future of the sport. The goal of this video was to capture the energy of the event, key moments from the discussions, and highlight FIFPRO's impact through engaging storytelling. This project allowed me to manage on-site video production, conduct interviews, and deliver a polished piece that was used across FIFPRO's communication channels.
