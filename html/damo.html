DAMÒ – AI Brand Video

Description

I recently collaborated with DAMÒ, an innovative brand focused on the integration of AI in fashion and lifestyle products. The video aimed to introduce DAMÒ’s cutting-edge approach to intelligent design and showcase how AI is shaping new consumer experiences.

Through creative storytelling and dynamic visuals, the video highlights DAMÒ’s unique identity and its vision for the future. This project involved concept development, production, and post-production, and strengthened my experience in producing brand content that bridges technology and creativity.

Roles

Direction – Edition – Color Grading – Marketing – Filming
