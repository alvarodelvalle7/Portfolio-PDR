Sporting CP Pink October Campaign x MWS

Description

I recently worked on a meaningful project for Sporting CP's Pink October campaign in collaboration with Matchwornshirt. My role encompassed conceptualizing, filming, and editing a video that aimed to raise awareness for breast cancer.

This campaign required a thoughtful and creative approach to convey its message effectively, balancing Sporting CP's identity with the campaign's emotional impact. The final video not only captured the spirit of solidarity but also showcased my ability to manage end-to-end content production for a cause-driven initiative.
