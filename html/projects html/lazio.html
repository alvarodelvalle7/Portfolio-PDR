MatchWornShirt – The Story of Mauro Patrizi

Description

In this project, I explored the vital, often unseen role of Mauro Patrizi, Lazio's Kitman. Filmed at the Formello training ground with support from matchwornshirt and S.S. Lazio, we captured Mauro’s stories and memories, highlighting the dedication behind every victory. This project brought to light the human element and passion that fuel football.
