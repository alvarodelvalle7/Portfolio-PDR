Luka Modric - Frame the game

Description

In this project, I collaborated with Frame the Game to create a promotional video featuring Luka Modric. The campaign showcased 100 framed jerseys of Modric, blending sports and art in a unique way. This video highlighted the craftsmanship and passion behind each framed piece, capturing Modric's involvement and the excitement of the fans.
